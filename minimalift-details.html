Overview

MinimaLift is a minimalistic workout tracking app that gives you the feel of using an oldschool pencil and paper to track your progress. This app removes all distractions in the gym, and lets you select apps to lock while you are doing your workout.

Key Features

  • Minimalistic workout tracking interface
  • App locking functionality to remove distractions
  • Progress tracking with simple, clean design
  • Workout session timer and tracking
  • Exercise library and personal records
  • Focus mode to enhance gym experience

Technologies Used

  • Swift for iOS app development
  • XCode for development environment
  • Firebase for data storage and user authentication
  • iOS App Lock APIs for distraction management
  • Core Data for local workout storage
